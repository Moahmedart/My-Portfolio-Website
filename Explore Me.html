Hi, I am from Kolkata and I have completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (formerly West Bengal University of Technology) in 2013.

I began my professional career in 2015 at the Reserve Bank of India (Central Bank of India), concurrently pursuing my Master's degree in Economics from IGNOU University, which I completed in 2019.

I am grateful to the bank for enriching me with knowledge and experiences. My communication skills, problem solving skills, and interpersonal skills have flourished. I was amazed by how Artificial Intelligence was facilitating various operations within the bank, ensuring smooth transactions.

My love for mathematics, playing with numbers, understanding graphs, statistics, and especially my fascination with technology have drawn me towards the field of Data Science. I am deeply passionate about Data Science and take pride in the hard work I've put in to assist others who are entering and striving to excel in this field. I regularly write blogs and create YouTube content to simplify complex topics for them.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, reading, writing, cooking, and mentoring people for free to help them build a bright future for themselves.
